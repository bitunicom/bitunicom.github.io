SHA-3 vs Keccak

To generate account address, Ethereum uses Keccak256 hash algorithm, takes public key as input, outputs 256bit(32 bytes, 64 hex characters) hash value, the last 40 characters(20 bytes, 160 bits) is kept as account address, the first 24 characters are dropped.
